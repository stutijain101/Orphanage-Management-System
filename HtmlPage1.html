FAQ


About Helping Hands

How can I reach Helping Hands?

First you need to come to Jaipur. You will find direct bus to Helping Hands in Sindhi camp. You can even board an auto rickshaw or a cab and ask the driver to come directly to Helping Hands.

At what time visitors can come to meet children in Helping Hands?

The preferred time for visitors to meet children in Helping Hands would be from 11am to 6pm. Make sure you have your adhar card and pan card with you while you are visiting. If you have a criminal record you won't be allowed to see the children. It is better to contact us ahead of time so that your time is not wasted.

How can I admit homeless children I see on streets in Helping Hands?

You just need to call us. We will send our volunteers to take the children to Helping Hands. You can also come to us to admit the homeless and needy children you see on streets.

Does Helping Hands take good care of orphans?

We are very selective in who we choose as volunteers to look after children. We don't let them become a permanent volunteer unless the senior volunteers are assured that they have the knack to do their job. They are very nurturing and friendly towards the children. We also make sure to give the children good food and education. We try our best to maintain cleanliness and the hygiene. You can always come and give us a visit if this answer is not able to satisfy you.

How can I donate to Helping Hands?

For donation, you need to manually come to Helping Hands. We accept all means of donation such as Cash, Check, Online Transactions(not through our website). Donations are always highly appreciated!

How can I contact Helping Hands?

You can give us a call, write an e-mail, contact us on social media platforms. All the details are provided in the footer of our website.

Adoption

What is the process for adoption after I get a token?

After you have been issued a token, you need to manually visit Helping Hands on the date specified. Give us a call beforehand for your convenience. Bring your adhaar card, Pan card, driving license, marriage certificate, salary slips of past 3 months, income certificate, health certificate. We will then ask you a few questions and tell you about all the legal proceedings.

What if it have been more than 2 days but I still have not received an email if I am eligible or not for adoption?

If it have been more than 2 days and yet you have received an email then it is likely that you were not eligible for adoption. If you think you have met all the criterias listed in adoption page of our website then feel free to write an email to us asking about the same. You can also give us a call.

What if i am not able to show up on the specified date for adoption?

In case you are not able to show up on the day you are expected to show up then please give us a call beforehand. Also, tell us the date when you can come. You registration would be disapproved if you fail to inform us within 2-3 days. To avoid that situation it is vital to inform us.

What if I want to cancel my registration for adoption?

In case you would like to cancel your registration for adoption then you can inform us through email. We will shortly send you the confirmation for cancellation.

Sponsorship

What does Sponsorship mean?

Sponsorship is a way to help the orphans without adopting them. It means that you can take care of their expenses such as education, clothes and so on. It would be a great help for children.

How long will the sponsorship last for?

It depends on how long you want to sponsor the child. We do recommend to sponsor them for at least an year. If you wish, you can sponsor them till they get independent or till someone adopts the child.

Can I sponsor more than one child?

Of course, you can! The registration form for sponsorship has a section where you tell how many kids you want to sponsor. You can write it there.

What if I want to cancel the sponsorship after I have been issued the token?

You can cancel your sponsorship by writing an email to us or giving us a call. You will receive an email shortly which confirms the cancellation.

Care Club Member

What is the work of a care club member?

The care club member of our orphanage is assigned the role to look after the working of the orphanage. They can volunteer to look after the kids, inspect and help with cleanliness and hygiene, cooking dishes and so on. A detailed lists of task will be issued upon arrival.

What if I am not able to show up on certains days when it is my shift?

If you can, please try to avoid that situation. It becomes very difficult for the orphanage when we are in short of staff. If the situation is too urgent and you cannot show up at any cost then please do notify us beforehand.

Will I have to give a test to confirm my place as a volunteer after my documents have been verified?

No such test is required. However, your work will be monitered for a week especially if you are doing tasks such as cooking and cleaning as it directly affects the health of children. The well being of children is our top priority, so observing your work would be required.

What will be the duration that I will serve as a volunteer?

It entirely depends on you. You are free to apply for volunteering and quit whenever you like. There is no strict duration.

Hobby Classes
What are the things volunteers can teach in hobby classes?

The volunteers can teach anything they specialize in. It could include dancing, singing, playing an instrument, drawing, painting, aerobics and so on. It can also include educational topics such as space, plants, animals and climate. Topics such as leadership and management are also more than welcome.

What if I do not have a certificate of the skill i specialize in?

You can still provide hobby classes even if you do not own any certificate. Skills are what matter the most. We will observe the classes you provide for a week and if we and the children are satisfied, we will love if you provide hobby classes regardless if you own any certificate or not.

How long will I have to provide hobby classes?

You can provide them for as long as you want. You can quit whenever you like and you can join whenever you like. We would highly appreciate if you could complete whatever you are teaching. Eg: If you are teaching the basics of singing then it would be wonderful if you finish teaching the basics. Leaving it midway would be bad.

How many hours would the class last?

It depends on your convenience. However, we do recommend that you at least provide the class for 30 minutes. The longest duration would ideally be 1.5 hrs because that is how much a human brain can compensate.

Are there any special documents I need to have when I manually visit the orphanage?

You must carry your aadhar card with you. It would be more convenient if you can bring your certificate, pan card and if you have experience tecahing/working in that field then bring documents related to do that.

Is there any age restriction on who can provide hobby classes?

The ideal age for providing hobby classes would be 18 and above. If you are below 18 then you would still be considered. However, we might not approve if you are less than 16 years old.
